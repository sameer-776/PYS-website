Why Prime Youth Summit?

Prime Youth Summit combines professional committee experiences with an energetic and welcoming atmosphere, ensuring that every delegate gains confidence, exposure, and meaningful learning.

The conference is designed to be beginner-friendly while still maintaining intellectual competitiveness. With carefully curated committees, experienced Executive Board members from across India, and delegates from diverse institutions, PYS creates an environment that balances debate, creativity, diplomacy, and collaboration.

What Makes PYS Different?

Premium venue experience

Interactive and engaging conference environment

The PYS Experience

Prime Youth Summit is more than formal committee sessions and debate. It is an experience built around conversations beyond placards, new friendships, collaborations, informal networking, and shared learning moments that extend beyond conference rooms.

At PYS, delegates do not simply participate in committees - they become part of a larger youth community driven by ambition, creativity, and impactful conversations.
